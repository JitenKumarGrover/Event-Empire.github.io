Rountine Services

Routine services do not correspond to day to day service but yes oil change is recommended after every 5000-7000 miles of driving. This may vary depending on the vehicle and its use. Every engine wears down eventually, but fresh oil prevents that from happening prematurely. Oil undergoes a thermal breakdown over time, leading to its failure and further engine friction increases. To avoid a very expensive trip to a repair shop, it is advisable to bring your truck for a routine service.
